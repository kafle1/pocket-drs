\newpage
\begin{center}
    \textbf{\LARGE ABSTRACT}
\end{center}
\vspace{1cm}
\noindent The "Timed - Medication Reminder Application" is designed to enhance medication adherence and health management for individuals with chronic conditions such as hypertension and diabetes. By providing timely reminders for medications and medical appointments, the app helps prevent complications  and improve overall health outcomes. It simplifies complex medication schedules, empowering users to manage their treatments independently and effectively, thereby improving quality of life. With customization options to cater to diverse patient needs, "Timed -  Medication Reminder Application" extends its impact to broader public health systems, promoting healthier lifestyles and empowered healthcare management.

\textbf{Keywords:} \textbf{Medication Reminder}, \textbf{Smartphone App}, \textbf{Appointment Scheduling}, \textbf{Digital Health}, \textbf{Personalized Care}.
